FAQ

Answers to your first questions.

Is Career Compass free?

Yes — students get free access to assessments, roadmaps and the AI chatbot. Premium unlocks mock interviews and mentorship.

How is my match calculated?

We combine academics, interests, personality (RIASEC + Big Five), skills, projects and real-time industry demand.

Can I use it after graduation?

Absolutely. Career Compass grows with you — from first internship to your next role.

Do recruiters use it?

Yes. Verified recruiters get a portal to post jobs and access placement-ready student profiles.
